Welcome to ENSG Digital Academy Where Innovation Meets Excellence and Tomorrow's Tech Leaders Are Born

Welcome to ENSG Digital Academy, where innovation, creativity, and excellence converge. Our state-of-the-art campus in Enugu provides a dynamic environment for students to explore coding, AI, robotics, and digital entrepreneurship through hands-on learning and real-world projects. Here, curiosity meets opportunity, challenges become stepping stones, and every student is empowered to turn ideas into impact. Guided by expert mentors and supported by cutting-edge technology, our learners are not just gaining skills—they are shaping the future of digital innovation. Join a vibrant community of visionaries and tech leaders, and take the first step on a journey where your talent drives change, creativity, and success. ENSG Digital Academy isn't just a school—it's the launchpad for tomorrow's digital pioneers.

ENSG Digital Academy Launches New AI Lab

Published on February 25, 2026

The ENSG Digital Academy has officially opened its state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence Lab, providing students with cutting-edge resources to explore AI, machine learning, and robotics.

The lab features interactive workstations, high-performance computing units, and mentorship programs to foster innovation among students. Workshops and training sessions will be held regularly to maximize hands-on experience.

With this new facility, the academy strengthens its commitment to producing globally competitive tech leaders and nurturing creative problem solvers.

Student Tech Expo Showcases Innovative Projects

Published on February 26, 2026

The annual Student Tech Expo at ENSG Digital Academy featured remarkable projects in app development, robotics, and digital solutions addressing real-world challenges.

Over 50 students presented their work to industry experts, receiving feedback and mentorship for future development. Several projects are now moving towards pilot implementation.

The expo reinforces the academy's focus on practical learning and community engagement, encouraging students to innovate beyond the classroom.

Scholarship Program Open for Applications

published on February 28, 2026

ENSG Digital Academy has announced its scholarship program for outstanding students in technology and digital innovation. The program covers tuition, mentorship, and access to special workshops.

Applicants are encouraged to submit portfolios, academic achievements, and project work demonstrating their skills and creativity. Selected scholars will also participate in leadership and career development programs.

This initiative aims to make high-quality digital education accessible to talented students, empowering them to lead in the tech world.
